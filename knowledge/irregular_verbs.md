## Learning Strategy for Irregular Verbs

Group verbs by pattern instead of memorizing alphabetically:
- No change: cut-cut-cut, put-put-put, cost-cost-cost
- Same past forms: bring-brought-brought, buy-bought-bought, think-thought-thought
- i/a/u pattern: begin-began-begun, drink-drank-drunk, swim-swam-swum

Learn all three forms together as one unit, and practice them in short
sentences rather than as a bare list.
